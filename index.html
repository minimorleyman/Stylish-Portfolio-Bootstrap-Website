A little about me

I was born to a wonderful family in the year 2000 and grew up in Sandy, Utah. I attended Hillcrest High School, where I excelled in both academics and the performing arts. After graduating and performing for a summer in Illinois, I embarked on one of the greatest and most transformative experiences of my life, serving as a full-time missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints in the Philippines Laoag Mission. After my mission I attended Utah State University for a year before transfering to Brigham Young University, where I continue to study today. In May of 2022, I married my best friend, Gloria Swenson, and together we aspire to change the world through the application of Experience Design principles to worldwide humanitarian efforts.
